# Momentum

<Row>
<Column>

## Linear momentum

SI unit: kg$\cdot$m/s

$$
\begin{align*}
\vec p &= m \vec v \\
\vec F_{net} &= \frac{d\vec p}{dt}
\end{align*}
$$

### Impulse

SI unit: N$\cdot$s

$$
\begin{align*}
\vec J &= \vec F_{net} \Delta t = \Delta\vec p \\
&= \int_{t_1}^{t_2}\vec F_{net} dt
\end{align*}
$$

</Column>
<Column>

## Angular Momentum

SI unit: kg$\cdot$m$^2$/s

$$
\begin{align*}
\vec L &= \vec r \times\vec p \\
\frac{d\vec L}{dt} &= \vec r \times\vec F = \vec \tau \\
L &= mvr\sin\theta \\
L &= I\omega
\end{align*}
$$

- $L = I\omega$ is true only if $\vec L$ is along the rotation axis
- Directions of $\vec\omega$ and $\vec L$ are the same, defined by the **right-hand thumb rule**

</Column>
</Row>

## Collisions

- **Total momentum** is conserved
- **Elastic collision**: KE and total energy conserved
- **Inelastic collision**: KE decreases during collision
- **Completely inelastic collision**: Objects stick together and KE decreases greatly during collision

### Elastic collision

$$
\begin{align*}
v_{A0} - v_{B0} = -(v_{A1} - v_{B1})
\end{align*}
$$

:::info

Special case: if object B is initially at rest

$$
\begin{align*}
v_{A1} &= \left(\frac{m_A - m_B}{m_A + m_B}\right) v_{A0} \\
v_{B1} &= \left(\frac{2m_A}{m_A + m_B}\right) v_{A0}
\end{align*}
$$

:::
