# Potential energy

## Conservative force

$$
\begin{align*}
\Delta U &= - \int_a^b\vec F\cdot d\vec s \\
F &= -\frac{dU}{dx}
\end{align*}
$$

## Gravitational PE

$$
\begin{align*}
U_g &= mgh
\end{align*}
$$

## Elastic PE

### Hooke's law (spring restoring force)

-   $k$: force constant of spring

$$
\begin{align*}
F &= -kx \\
U_e &= \frac{1}{2}kx^2
\end{align*}
$$

### Horizontal glider connected to a spring

$$
\begin{align*}
s = -\frac{\mu_k mg}{k} \pm \sqrt{\left(\frac{\mu_k mg}{k}\right)^2 + \frac{mv^2}{k}}
\end{align*}
$$
