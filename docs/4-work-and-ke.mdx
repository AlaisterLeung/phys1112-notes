# Work and kinetic energy

## Kinetic energy

Translational:
$$
K_{tr} = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
$$

[Rotational](/7-moment-of-inertia):
$$
K_{rot} = \frac{1}{2}I\omega^2
$$

## Work done

Work-energy theorem:
$$
W = \Delta E = \Delta K + \Delta U
$$

Constant force:
$$
W = \vec F \cdot \vec s = |F||s|\cos\theta
$$

Varying force:
$$
W = \int \vec F \cdot d \vec x
$$

[Torque](/7-moment-of-inertia#torque):
$$
W = \int \tau d\theta
$$

### Power

$$
P = \frac{dW}{dt}
$$

Translational:
$$
P = \vec F \cdot \vec v
$$

[Rotational](/7-moment-of-inertia#torque):
$$
P = \tau\omega
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
W_T &= 0 \\
W_F &= \int_0^\theta F\cos\theta dl = mgr(1-\cos\theta) \\
W_W &= \int_0^\theta mg\cos(\frac{\pi}{2}+\theta) dl = -mgr(1-\cos\theta) \\
W_{net} &= W_T + W_F + W_W = 0
\end{align*}
$$

- $W_T$: work done by tension
- $W_F$: work done by external push
- $W_w$: work done by weight
- $r$: radius of the curved path
- $l$: displacement of the person
